/bio

precis

Jingyin He is an experimental sound and integrated media artist, researcher and educator. His artistic focus is driven by the search for computational creativity in contemporary sonic and visual art practices: through the development of new interfaces and interaction schemes.

He is currently a Ph.D candidate at the Victoria University of Wellington working towards his doctoral dissertation on the analysis and modeling of plucked string instrument performance using multi-modal sensor systems and mechatronic musical instruments. Most recently, Jingyin completed a New Zealand tour of the theatre production, High Mountain, Flowing Water , as the main visual designer in charge of animation, typography, live projection mapping and audiovisual interaction.
